  11. Which of the following rate laws represents a unimolecular primary reaction?

    12. Step-by-step reasoning

    Step 1: Recall the definitions

    For primary reactions:

    • A unimolecular reaction has an overall order of 1.
      • (e.g. Rate = k[A])
    • A bimolecular reaction has an overall order of 2.
      • (e.g. Rate = k[A]2, or Rate = k[A][B])
    • A termolecular reaction has an overall order of 3.
      • (e.g. Rate = k[A]3, or Rate = k[A]2[B], or Rate = K[A][B][C])
