    27. Guided Problem

    Which law of thermodynamics states that the total entropy of the universe increases for any spontaneous process?

  27. Reference: Laws of Thermodynamics
    • Zeroth Law: If two systems are each in thermal equilibrium with a third system, they are in thermal equilibrium with each other.
    • First Law: Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transferred or converted from one form to another.
    • Second Law: The total entropy of the universe increases for any spontaneous process.
    • Third Law: The entropy of a perfect crystalline substance is zero at absolute zero temperature.
