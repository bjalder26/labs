Question 25: Make the smallest amount of water molecules. How many of each reactant were used?

The mole ratio for this reaction is written as:


2 mol H2: 1 mol O2: 2 mol of H2O


Now notice that the chemical reaction (like the sandwich recipe) is


2H2 + O2 → 2H2O


The mole ratio matches the coefficients of the balanced chemical equation!
