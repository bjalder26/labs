1) The height of a lab bench is approximately 90.0 cm. Convert this to feet (ft).
cm → ft

2) Measure the width of a pencil or pen in millimeters (mm) and convert to miles (mi).
mm → mi

3) Measure the mass of a pen or pencil in grams (g) and convert it to pounds (lbs).
g → lbs

4) Measure the volume of water that can fit in a cup using a graduated cylinder (mL) and convert to gallons (gal).
mL → gal

5) Measure the mass of a penny in grams (g) and convert to slugs.
g → slugs

6) Measure the length of a lab bench in inches (in) and convert to yards (yd).
in → yd

7) Measure the time (s) it takes to recite the alphabet and convert to days. (note: final answer should be in scientific notation)
s → days

8) Measure the time (s) it takes to walk down the hallway and convert it to weeks. (note: final answer should be in scientific notation)
s → weeks

9) Measure the height of a classmate in inches (in) and convert it to millimeters (mm).
in → mm

10) Measure the volume of a penny in milliliters (mL) and convert it to liters (L).
mL → L

11) Measure the volume of a book in centimeters cubed (cm3) (length x width x height) and convert it to liters (L).
cm3 → L

12) Convert your age in years (y) to hours (hrs).
y → hr
